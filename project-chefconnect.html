ChefConnect

A private chef booking platform connecting talented chefs with clients for in-home dining experiences.

ChefConnect is a marketplace that connects private chefs with clients looking for personalized in-home dining experiences. Chefs create detailed profiles with their cuisine specialties, sample menus, and availability calendars.

Clients can browse chefs by cuisine type, price range, and location. The booking system handles everything from initial inquiry to payment and post-event reviews. Each booking includes custom menu planning, dietary accommodation notes, and ingredient sourcing coordination.

I built a review and rating system that builds trust in the marketplace, along with a secure payment gateway that holds funds in escrow until the event is completed. The platform also includes a messaging system for chefs and clients to coordinate event details. ChefConnect launched with 50 chefs in three cities and expanded to ten cities within six months.

Tech Stack

React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Stripe Connect, Twilio, AWS S3

Key Features

Chef profiles, Booking system, Escrow payments, Reviews & ratings, In-app messaging
