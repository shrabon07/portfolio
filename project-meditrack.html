MediTrack

A healthcare management system with patient portals, appointment scheduling, and EHR integration.

MediTrack is a comprehensive healthcare management platform designed for a multi-location clinic network. It streamlines patient intake, appointment scheduling, billing, and electronic health record management into a single unified interface.

The system features a patient portal where users can book appointments, view medical records, communicate with providers, and receive prescription refill reminders. On the provider side, doctors and nurses have dashboards showing daily schedules, patient histories, and clinical decision support tools.

Built with security and compliance as top priorities, MediTrack implements HIPAA-compliant data handling, role-based access control, and encrypted data storage. The appointment scheduling module reduced no-shows by 35% through automated SMS and email reminders.

Tech Stack

React, Node.js, PostgreSQL, Twilio, HIPAA-compliant infrastructure

Key Features

Patient portal, Appointment scheduling, EHR management, Role-based access, Automated reminders
