A comprehensive fitness tracking app with personalized workout plans, progress analytics, exercise libraries, and social challenges to keep you motivated.

Live DemoAll Projects
Overview

Track More.
Achieve More.

PulseFit is a cross-platform fitness tracking application designed to help users reach their health goals through data-driven insights. Built with React Native for a native feel on both iOS and Android, PulseFit offers personalized workout recommendations powered by user data, detailed progress visualizations, an extensive exercise library with video guides, and community challenges that make fitness social and fun.
