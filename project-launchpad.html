LaunchPad

A dynamic product launch page with countdown timer, waitlist management, and social proof integration.

LaunchPad is a pre-launch marketing page built for a tech startup gearing up for their product release. The page features an interactive countdown timer, a waitlist signup system, and social proof elements that generated buzz before launch day.

The page includes a feature teaser section with animated reveals, an email waitlist with referral tracking, and a live counter showing waitlist signups. Each visitor gets a unique referral link, turning early adopters into advocates. The system sent automated drip emails to keep subscribers engaged.

On launch day, the countdown triggered a smooth transition to the main product page. The campaign built a waitlist of 5,000+ subscribers before launch, and 40% of them converted to paid users within the first week.

Tech Stack

Next.js, Redis, SendGrid, Framer Motion, PostgreSQL

Key Features

Countdown timer, Referral system, Waitlist management, Drip email campaigns, Launch transition
