LaunchPad

A high-converting product launch page template with countdown timers, email capture, and social proof elements.

Launch Live Demo

LaunchPad is a meticulously crafted product launch page designed to maximize conversions. It combines psychological triggers like scarcity countdowns, social proof notifications, and urgent call-to-actions with stunning visual design.

Built with Gatsby for blazing-fast performance and integrated with Netlify Forms and Mailchimp for email capture, the page is fully optimized for SEO and conversions. Google Analytics tracking is built in from the start.

Key features include a dynamic countdown timer with timezone auto-detection, optimized email signup forms with auto-responders, live visitor counters and testimonial carousels, and built-in A/B testing for data-driven decisions.

Tech Stack

Gatsby, Netlify Forms, Mailchimp, Google Analytics, Styled Components

Key Features

Countdown timer, Email capture with auto-responders, Social proof notifications, A/B testing support
